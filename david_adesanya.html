David Adesanya

BEHIND HOME

@behindbeijing

At present, we are the most globalised we have ever been in the history of humanity, yet so disconnected. I found this paradox to be a pressing discussion in LDN and wondered...

What if a consistent conversation could emerge? Not from the politicians, not from the mayors or county leaders, but from the people on the ground now? What then?

With a camera, globe and those intentions in mind, I took to the streets to discover what grounded the people of London when the struggles and pressures of life unsettled their foundation.
